Current Project

Kisahloka

Kisahloka is an online platform for folklore stories that provides a captivating reading experience with attractive and aesthetic visuals.

Our tagline is :

LIVE THE STORY, PRESERVE THE CULTURE

Which portrays folklore stories as a window that brings us into the richness of culture and its magical world.

In Kisahloka, users can find various types of stories, ranging from fiction to non-fiction, all presented with an engaging layout and complemented by captivating images, illustrations, and graphics.

With intuitive navigation, users can easily explore various stories and find content that matches their interests and preferences.
